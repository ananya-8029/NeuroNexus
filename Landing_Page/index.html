The wonders of the world include both ancient and modern marvels. These iconic landmarks stand as testaments to the creativity, engineering prowess, and cultural significance of civilizations throughout history.
These were remarkable, humanly-constructed landmarks from ancient, classical civilisations.

The Seven Wonders of the World are a group of places around the globe that are considered to be of great importance. These are: The Colosseum in Italy, Petra in Jordan, Chichén Itzá in Mexico, Christ the Redeemer in Brazil, Machu Picchu in Peru, Taj Mahal in India and The Great Wall of China.
